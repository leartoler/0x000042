Possible futures

Olinka, the utopian city envisioned by Dr. Atl, where creative and visionary minds would live. These 3D models, created using Feather 3D software, offer an idea of how the city might look in the future, after a catastrophic event. The concept was designed to be developed as a video game for the Centro de Cultura Digital in Mexico City in 2025.
